Ping Pong Prototype

In this assignment we programmed a microcontroller to display ping pong
and learned how to use electricals to connect a potentiometer as a controller/input.

Wearable Technology Prototype (Fire Arm)

To start this prototype we researched wearable technology and sketched out concepts.
Once solidified on a concept we prepared schematic diagrams for the elctronics of our prototype. Next we programmed
python into our microcontrollers and debugged any practical issues for proper demos.

Air Quality Monitoring Project

To create an air quality monitor we first learned to use IoT systems and embed networking.
We then created and printed our cases, wired up our microcontrollers, and left the sensors outside to measure
the air quality to real time graph data.
